WHAT DO DJS ACTUALLY DO?

Based on the Pioneer DJ DDJ-REV1-N Serato Performance DJ Controller.

Enjoy!

Image taken by Kate Wiggins.

  • Press the 'PLAY/PAUSE' buttons to play either song.
  • Use the vertical sliders to adjust the volume.
  • Press the 'CUE' button to play both songs at the same time.
  • Use the crossfader to fade in/out a song.
  • Press on the 'JOGWHEEL' to create a record scratch effect.
  • Press the 'HIGH' button to put a high pass filter on song 1.
  • Press the 'LOW' button to put a low pass filter on song 2.
